Clara Guzman

Artist, Magazine Editor, Graphic Designer

I am a senior at Noble and Greenough School and, to me, art is what I look forward to at the end of a long day (other than a nap).

I lose myself to art, consumed with lines, curves and most importantly colors. My favorite medium is pastel because I am physically touching color - blending it and swirling it on the page. I have studied watercolor, acrylic and digital painting, magazine design and figure drawing. Some of my favorite classes include Figure Drawing at School of Visual Arts, Observational Painting at Mass School of Art, Portraiture at Acton Art School and Digital Sculpting at Gnomon School of Visual Effects and Animation.
